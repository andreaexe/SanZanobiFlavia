Location Information

Nearby Attractions

  • Florence Cathedral: Only a 12-minute walk away.
  • Accademia Gallery: See Michelangelo's David, just a 7-minute walk.
  • Piazza della Signoria: Around a 17-minute walk.
  • Ponte Vecchio: A 19-minute walk through the historic center.

Nearby Services

  • Supermarket: Just a 2-minute walk, ideal for quick groceries.
  • Pharmacy: Brizio Massei Pharmacy is only 1 minute from the property.
  • Restaurants and Cafés: Plenty of local dining and café options nearby.
  • ATM: Located a few minutes away, in Piazza San Marco.

How to Reach Us

Our holiday home is easily accessible by several means:

  • By Train: From Santa Maria Novella station, the property is about a 10-minute walk.
  • By Air: From Florence Airport, take the T2 tram to the central station, then walk.
  • By Car: Follow signs to the city center. Parking is available near the property (booking recommended).
  • By Bus: Several bus lines stop nearby, including links to the metropolitan area.
